Research Areas

My research focuses on addressing real-world computational challenges using intelligent optimization, deep learning, and safety-driven modelling. I work extensively on meta-heuristic algorithm design, intelligent routing systems, and advanced medical image analysis.

Meta-heuristic Optimization

Developed the **Slender Loris Optimization Algorithm (SLOA)** ‚Äî a novel nature-inspired meta-heuristic for global optimization. Applied in transportation routing, load balancing, and safety-aware decision-making frameworks.

Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)

Designed safety-aware navigation frameworks using POI-driven routing, LSTM-Attention models for dynamic safety scoring, and optimized route planning for urban travelers, including emergency response systems.

Medical Image Processing

Research on multimodal MRI/DTI segmentation, tumour delineation, and deep learning fusion using transformer-based architectures for improved diagnostics.

Cloud Security & Data Integrity

Worked on secure deduplication techniques, client-side encrypted data models, and optimization-based security frameworks for distributed cloud systems.

Ongoing Projects

  • WOMEN_SLOA: A next-generation safety navigation system for women traveling at night. Integrates POI-based routing, deep-learning safety scores, and SLOA-based route optimization.
  • Multimodal Medical Image Segmentation: Development of transformer-based deep learning pipelines for tumour detection and radiological analysis.
  • Meta-heuristic Benchmarking: Comparative performance evaluation of GTBO, SLOA, PSO, Firefly, and other optimization algorithms.
